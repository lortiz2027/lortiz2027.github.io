Instructions

Welcome to the Speed Typing Game! The objective of the game is to type the given sentence as quickly and accurately as possible.

Here's how to play:

  1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  2. Type the sentence displayed in the input field.
  3. As you type, the characters will change color to indicate correctness.
  4. Once you've typed the entire sentence correctly, a new sentence will appear automatically.
  5. Continue typing until the timer runs out.
  6. After the game ends, your score will be displayed.
  7. Remember your score and set your personal record.
  8. Click "Retry" to play again or "Home Page" to return to the homepage.
